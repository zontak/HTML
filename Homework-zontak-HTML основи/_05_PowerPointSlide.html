Heading And Paragraphs

  • Heading Tags: <h1> – <h6>
    • <h1>Heading 1 </h1>
    <h2>Sub heading 2 </h2>
    <h3>Sub heading 3 </h3>
  • Paragraph Tags: <p>
    • <p>Sub heading 2 </p>
    <p>Sub heading 3 </p>
  • Sections: <div> and <span>
    • <div style ="background: skyblue;">
    This is a div
    </div>
